Privacy Policy for Reshuffle

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to Reshuffle ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email, name, and other profile information
  • Social media connections: If you choose to connect social accounts (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.), we collect profile information from these platforms with your permission
  • Usage data: We collect data about how you interact with the App

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process social media connections when you authorize them
  • Personalize your experience within the App
  • Communicate with you about updates and features

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • For legal purposes, if required by law or to protect our rights

Social Media Connections

When you connect your social media accounts (like Instagram or LinkedIn), we only access the information you have explicitly authorized through the OAuth permission process. We request minimal permissions necessary to provide the App's functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights concerning your personal data, including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: support@reshuffleapp.com
